## Dashboard Overview

The admin dashboard is your command center for monitoring store performance and making data-driven decisions.

## Key Metrics

### Today's Performance

Real-time metrics updated every 5 minutes:

- **Today's Revenue** - Total sales amount
- **Orders Count** - Number of orders received
- **Conversion Rate** - Percentage of visitors who purchased
- **Average Order Value (AOV)** - Average amount per order
- **Active Carts** - Customers currently shopping

### This Week

Weekly performance indicators:

- **Weekly Revenue** - 7-day sales total with trend
- **Order Volume** - Number of orders vs last week
- **New Customers** - New registrations this week
- **Top Products** - Best-selling items
- **Abandoned Carts** - Percentage of abandoned carts

### This Month

Monthly overview:

- **Monthly Revenue** - Current month vs last month
- **Growth Rate** - Percentage change
- **Customer Retention** - Repeat customer rate
- **Product Performance** - Category breakdown
- **Geographical Sales** - Sales by region

## Dashboard Widgets

### Sales Chart

Interactive revenue chart:

- **Time Range** - Today, 7 days, 30 days, 90 days, year
- **Comparison** - Compare with previous period
- **Export** - Download as CSV or PDF
- **Granularity** - Hour, day, week, month views

Products requiring attention:

- **Out of Stock** (Red) - Requires immediate restocking
- **Low Stock** (Orange) - Below reorder point
- **Available** (Green) - Adequate inventory

### Sales Reports

Generate detailed sales reports:

- **Sales by Product** - Revenue per product

- **Sales by Customer** - Customer purchase history

### Financial Reports

Track your finances:

- **Revenue Report** - Gross revenue, net revenue
- **Tax Report** - Tax collected by region
- **Refunds Report** - Refunded orders and reasons

### Inventory Reports

Monitor your stock:

## Troubleshooting

### Dashboard Not Loading

1. Clear browser cache
2. Disable browser extensions
3. Try incognito mode
4. Check internet connection
5. Contact support

### Data Not Updating

1. Refresh the page
2. Check date range filters
3. Verify cache settings
4. Review recent changes
5. Check system status

### Missing Widgets

1. Click "Customize Dashboard"
2. Add missing widgets
3. Reset to default layout
4. Clear preferences
5. Contact support
