## How to Submit an Idea

1. **Check existing ideas** - Search the [Ideas repository](https://github.com/cartinophp/ideas) to see if someone already suggested something similar
2. **Create a new discussion** - Open a new discussion in the Ideas category
3. **Describe your idea** - Explain the feature, use case, and benefits
4. **Engage with the community** - Discuss with other users and maintainers

## What Makes a Good Feature Request?

::u-tabs
---
items:
  - label: Problem
    slot: problem
  - label: Solution
    slot: solution
  - label: Use Case
    slot: use-case
  - label: Examples
    slot: examples
---

#problem
### Describe the Problem

Start by clearly explaining what problem you're trying to solve or what limitation you've encountered.

**Good Example:**
> "When managing a multi-brand store, there's no way to filter products by multiple brands simultaneously in the admin panel."

#solution
### Propose a Solution

Suggest how you think the feature should work. Be specific but open to alternatives.

**Good Example:**
> "Add a multi-select filter for brands in the product list view, similar to how category filtering works."

#use-case
### Explain the Use Case

Help us understand when and why this feature would be useful.

**Good Example:**
> "This would help store managers with 50+ brands quickly find products during bulk updates and inventory audits."

#examples
### Provide Examples

Show examples from other platforms or mockups if possible.

**Good Example:**
> "Shopify has a similar feature in their admin panel - screenshot attached."
::

## Voting & Prioritization

The community can vote on ideas using GitHub reactions (üëç). We consider:

- **Community interest** - Number of upvotes and comments
- **Impact** - How many users would benefit
- **Effort** - Development complexity
- **Alignment** - Fits with Cartino's vision and architecture

## Popular Ideas

Some of the most requested features from our community:

- **Multi-warehouse routing** - Automatic order routing based on inventory location
- **Subscription products** - Recurring billing and subscription management
- **Advanced reporting** - Custom dashboards and analytics
- **B2B features** - Quote management, credit terms, bulk ordering
- **Marketplace support** - Multi-vendor marketplace functionality

::callout{icon="i-lucide-sparkles" color="amber"}
These are community suggestions and may or may not be on the roadmap. Vote on [GitHub Ideas](https://github.com/cartinophp/ideas) to show your interest!
::
