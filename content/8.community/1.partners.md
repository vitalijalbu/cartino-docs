## Hiring a Partner

### How to Choose

Consider these factors:

**Budget:**

- Freelancers: $50-200/hr
- Bronze partners: $75-150/hr
- Silver partners: $100-200/hr
- Gold partners: $150-300/hr

**Project Size:**

- Small (< $25k): Freelancer or Bronze
- Medium ($25k-$100k): Silver
- Large ($100k+): Gold
- Enterprise: Gold with proven experience

**Timeline:**

- Rush projects: Partners with availability
- Standard timeline: Any tier
- Ongoing: Consider retainer agreements

**Location:**

- Time zone overlap for communication
- Local vs remote preferences
- Language requirements

### Questions to Ask

**Experience:**

1. How many Cartino projects have you completed?
2. Can you share similar project examples?
3. What's your largest e-commerce project?
4. Do you have experience with [specific feature]?

**Process:**

1. What's your development process?
2. How do you handle communication?
3. What's your typical project timeline?
4. How do you handle scope changes?

**Support:**

1. Do you provide ongoing maintenance?
2. What's your support response time?
3. Do you offer training for our team?
4. What happens after launch?

**Technical:**

1. What's your tech stack preference?
2. How do you ensure code quality?
3. Do you follow Cartino best practices?
4. How do you handle testing?
