::migration-story

timeline: 2 weeks
---

**Why we migrated:**
- Transaction fees adding up ($3.3K/month)
- Hit customization limits
- Needed custom fields for sustainability data

**The migration:**
- CSV export from Shopify
- Import to Cartino in one evening
- Theme rebuilt in 1 week
- Went live with zero downtime

**Results:**
- Saving $40K/year in fees
- Can now track sustainability metrics
- Customers love the improved product pages
