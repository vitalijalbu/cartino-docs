## Success Stories

::success-story
---
company: Organic Threads
logo: /images/logos/organic-threads.svg

author: Sarah Johnson
role: Founder & CEO

timeframe: 12 months

### The Challenge
Organic Threads needed a way to display detailed sustainability information for each product—certifications, carbon footprint, and material sources—without hiring a developer.

### The Solution
Using Cartino's Blueprint system, they created custom fields for all sustainability data. No database migrations, no coding required.

### The Results
- **400% revenue growth** in 12 months
- **85% of customers** reference sustainability data
- **50% reduction** in customer service inquiries
- **Expanded to 3 markets** using multi-site features

company: TechGear Pro

quote: We migrated 50,000 products from Magento to Cartino in a weekend. The performance improvement was immediate and dramatic.
author: Mike Chen
role: CTO
improvement: 10x faster
metric: Page Load Time
image: /images/testimonials/mike-chen.jpg
---

### The Challenge
TechGear was struggling with slow page loads on their Magento store. With 50,000 products, their site took 3-5 seconds to load product pages.

### The Solution
Migrated to Cartino and used Meilisearch for search, Redis for caching, and Cartino's optimized queries.

### The Results
- **10x faster page loads** (500ms average)
- **65% increase in conversion rate**
- **40% reduction in bounce rate**
- **$500K savings** on server costs annually
