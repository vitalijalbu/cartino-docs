## What's Covered

### Store Management

- **Dashboard** - Overview of your store performance
- **Products** - Manage your product catalog
- **Orders** - Process and fulfill customer orders
- **Customers** - Manage customer accounts and groups
- **Inventory** - Track stock levels across locations
- **Discounts** - Create promotions and coupon codes

### Store Configuration

- **Settings** - Configure store information and policies
- **Shipping** - Set up shipping zones and rates
- **Payments** - Configure payment gateways
- **Taxes** - Set up tax rates and rules
- **Notifications** - Configure email templates

### Advanced Features

- **Reports** - Analyze sales and performance
- **Marketing** - Email campaigns and promotions
- **Multi-Site** - Manage multiple storefronts
- **User Roles** - Control staff permissions
