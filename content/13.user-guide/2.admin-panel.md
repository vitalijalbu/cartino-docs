# Admin Panel Overview

The Cartino Admin Panel is your command center for managing your online store. This guide will familiarize you with the interface and key features.

## Accessing the Admin Panel

### Login

1. Navigate to your store's admin URL: `https://yourstore.com/admin`
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

![Admin Login Screen](~/assets/screenshots/admin-login.png)

::alert{type="warning"}
**Forgot your password?** Click "Forgot Password?" to receive a reset link via email.
::

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

If 2FA is enabled:
1. Enter your email and password
2. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app
3. Click **Verify**

![Two-Factor Authentication](~/assets/screenshots/2fa-prompt.png)

## Dark Mode

Toggle between light and dark themes:

1. Click your profile picture in the top right
2. Click **Dark Mode** toggle
3. Theme changes immediately

![Dark Mode Toggle](~/assets/screenshots/dark-mode-toggle.png)

**Or** use the keyboard shortcut: `Cmd+Shift+D` (Mac) or `Ctrl+Shift+D` (Windows)

## Customizing the Admin

### Personal Preferences

Configure how the admin panel works for you:

1. Go to **Your Profile â†’ Preferences**
2. Adjust settings:
   - **Language** - Choose your preferred language
   - **Timezone** - Set your local timezone
   - **Date Format** - MM/DD/YYYY or DD/MM/YYYY
   - **Number Format** - Decimal separator, thousands separator
   - **Dashboard Layout** - Choose which widgets to display

![Admin Preferences](~/assets/screenshots/admin-preferences.png)

### Dashboard Widgets

Customize your dashboard:

1. Click **Customize Dashboard**
2. Drag and drop widgets to reorder
3. Click the **X** to remove widgets
4. Click **Add Widget** to add new ones
5. Click **Save Layout**

![Dashboard Customization](~/assets/screenshots/dashboard-customize.png)

Available widgets:
- Sales Chart
- Recent Orders
- Top Products
- Revenue by Channel
- Conversion Funnel
- Customer Locations
- Low Stock Alerts

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Can't Login

1. Clear browser cache and cookies
2. Try in an incognito/private window
3. Reset password if needed
4. Check with your admin if account is active

#### Dashboard Not Loading

1. Refresh the page (F5)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check internet connection
4. Contact support if issue persists

#### Missing Menu Items

You may not have permission to access certain sections. Contact your store admin to adjust your role permissions.
